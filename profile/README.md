# Hi there, this is Forgemind 👋

Forgemind builds AI agentic systems for businesses. We connect workflow automation, messaging APIs, and data pipelines so that reaching, teaching, and tracking happens reliably and at scale — with full control over your own data and deployments.
As a Meta Verified Business Partner, we work to the standards that serious messaging at scale demands.
We're primarily a service business — we design, build, and run these systems for our clients end to end. We deploy automation across every department in a business:

- 🤖 We build AI agentic business systems that automate every layer — social media, sales and marketing, service, and internal task management.
- ⚙️ We orchestrate workflow automation — routing, scheduling, and managing everything end to end.
- 💬 We work deeply with the WhatsApp Business API for templated, per-recipient messaging and engagement tracking.
- 📊 We build dashboards and software that turn automated data into insight you can see and act on.

Rather than relying on existing paid SaaS tools, we build agents around a defined set of tasks — and from there, an entire foundation and system on top of it that a business can own outright.
